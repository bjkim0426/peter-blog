About Me

From Curiosity to Code: My Journey in Software Development.

The first programming language I learned was Python, through the freeCodeCamp.org YouTube channel. After grasping the basics of Python, I enrolled in the 100 Days of Code bootcamp taught by Dr. Angela Yu on Udemy, where I deepened my knowledge of Python. Completing the course, I realized how much I enjoyed coding, especially the ability to bring my software ideas to life. This passion led me to pursue a career in computer science, and I’m excited to continue developing my skills and contributing to innovative projects.
